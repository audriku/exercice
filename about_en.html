About us

Since 1976, our restaurant offers you dishes and drinks made out of local products, made in Brittany. Since its first location in Saint-Malo, our team of producers and agricultural partners have always been there to offer you the best. Our brewing expertise has been passed down through generations. Today, we have seven locations in Brittany.
